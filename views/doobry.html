doobry is a free and fully open source developer & administrator tool for DocumentDb

Works on Windows 7, 8 and 10, just download the installer, install once and the application will automatically keep itself updated.

Develop and test your DocumentDb SQL scripts, query your databases, add, copy and remove documents.
